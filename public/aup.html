1. INTRODUCTION

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of Kintsugi AI services. This policy is designed to protect our community, maintain platform integrity, and ensure compliance with applicable laws. Violations of this AUP may result in account suspension or termination.

By using Kintsugi AI, you agree to comply with this AUP in addition to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2. PROHIBITED CONTENT

You may NOT upload, share, transmit, or generate content that:

2.1 Illegal Content

  • Violates any local, state, national, or international law
  • Promotes or facilitates illegal activities
  • Contains child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploits minors
  • Infringes on intellectual property rights (copyright, trademark, patents)
  • Violates privacy rights or discloses personal information without consent

2.2 Harmful Content

  • Promotes violence, terrorism, or extremism
  • Contains graphic violence or gore without legitimate purpose
  • Encourages self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders
  • Depicts animal cruelty or abuse
  • Contains malware, viruses, or malicious code
  • Promotes dangerous activities or challenges

2.3 Hateful Content

  • Promotes hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics
  • Advocates for violence against individuals or groups
  • Contains symbols, imagery, or language associated with hate groups
  • Engages in targeted harassment or bullying

2.4 Adult Content

  • Sexually explicit content or pornography
  • Non-consensual intimate imagery (revenge porn)
  • Sexual solicitation or prostitution
  • Sexualized content involving minors in any form

2.5 Deceptive Content

  • Misinformation or disinformation designed to deceive
  • Impersonation of individuals, organizations, or entities
  • Phishing attempts or social engineering
  • Fraudulent schemes or scams
  • Manipulated media (deepfakes) without clear disclosure

3. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

3.1 Platform Abuse

  • Spam: Sending unsolicited bulk messages or repetitive content
  • Automated Access: Using bots, scrapers, or unauthorized automation tools
  • System Manipulation: Circumventing usage limits, rate limits, or token restrictions
  • Account Abuse: Creating multiple accounts to evade restrictions or bans
  • Resource Exploitation: Excessive use that degrades service for others

3.2 Security Violations

  • Attempting unauthorized access to accounts, systems, or data
  • Probing, scanning, or testing vulnerabilities without authorization
  • Interfering with or disrupting service availability (DoS, DDoS)
  • Bypassing security measures or authentication mechanisms
  • Reverse engineering or decompiling our software

3.3 Misuse of AI Features

  • Generating content that violates this AUP using AI tools
  • Attempting to jailbreak or manipulate AI models
  • Using AI to impersonate real individuals without disclosure
  • Creating deepfakes for malicious purposes
  • Extracting or replicating AI model weights or parameters
  • Using AI-generated content for illegal activities

4. USER BEHAVIOR STANDARDS

4.1 Respectful Communication

  • Treat all users with respect and dignity
  • Do not engage in harassment, threats, or intimidation
  • Respect others' privacy and personal boundaries
  • Do not share private conversations without consent

4.2 Authentic Use

  • Use your real identity or a clearly identified pseudonym
  • Do not impersonate others or create fake accounts
  • Accurately represent your affiliations and credentials
  • Do not manipulate platform metrics (likes, views, followers)

4.3 Commercial Use

  • Personal use is permitted; commercial use requires approval
  • Do not resell or redistribute our services
  • Do not use the platform for unauthorized advertising
  • Affiliate marketing and MLM schemes are prohibited

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

You must respect intellectual property rights when using Kintsugi AI:

  • Only upload content you own or have permission to use
  • Do not share copyrighted material without authorization
  • Provide proper attribution when required
  • Respond to DMCA takedown notices promptly
  • Do not use AI to generate content that infringes on IP rights

To report copyright infringement, contact dmca@kintsugi-ai.com

6. ENFORCEMENT

6.1 Violation Consequences

Violations of this AUP may result in:

  • Warning: First-time minor violations
  • Content Removal: Deletion of violating content
  • Feature Restriction: Temporary suspension of specific features
  • Account Suspension: Temporary account deactivation (7-30 days)
  • Account Termination: Permanent ban for serious or repeat violations
  • Legal Action: Reporting to authorities for illegal activities

6.2 Zero Tolerance

Immediate permanent ban without warning for:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Credible threats of violence
  • Terrorism or violent extremism
  • Human trafficking or exploitation
  • Coordinated harassment campaigns

7. REPORTING VIOLATIONS

Help us maintain a safe community by reporting violations:

7.1 How to Report

  • In-App Reporting: Use the "Report" button on content or profiles
  • Email: Send details to abuse@kintsugi-ai.com
  • Emergency: For immediate threats, contact local authorities first

7.2 What to Include

  • Username or profile of violator
  • Description of the violation
  • Screenshots or evidence (if safe to collect)
  • Date and time of incident
  • Links to specific content (if applicable)

7.3 Our Response

We review all reports within 24-48 hours. We may:

  • Investigate the reported content or behavior
  • Take appropriate enforcement action
  • Notify you of the outcome (privacy permitting)
  • Report illegal activity to authorities

8. SECURITY AND PRIVACY

Protect your account and others' privacy:

  • Keep your password secure and confidential
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Do not share your account credentials
  • Log out from shared or public devices
  • Report suspicious activity or unauthorized access immediately
  • Do not attempt to access others' accounts
  • Respect privacy settings and consent

9. POLICY UPDATES

We may update this AUP to reflect changes in technology, law, or community standards. Updates will be communicated through:

  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Email notification to registered users
  • In-app notifications for material changes
