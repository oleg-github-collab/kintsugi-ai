1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Kintsugi AI ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

By accessing or using Kintsugi AI, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Display name, avatar, bio, preferences
  • Communication Data: Messages, translations, AI chat conversations
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe (we do not store full card details)
  • User Content: Files, images, videos, and other content you upload

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent, interaction patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (not precise GPS)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session tokens, preferences, analytics data

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: AI chat and image generation (subject to OpenAI's privacy policy)
  • DeepL: Translation services (subject to DeepL's privacy policy)
  • 100ms: Video conferencing infrastructure
  • Stripe: Payment processing (subject to Stripe's privacy policy)

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Service Provision: Deliver, maintain, and improve our services
  • Personalization: Customize your experience and preferences
  • Communication: Send service updates, notifications, and support messages
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns and improve features
  • Security: Detect fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • AI Training: Improve AI models (conversations are anonymized)

4. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist in operations (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • AI Service Providers: OpenAI and DeepL for AI and translation features
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Public Information: Content you choose to make public (stories, public groups)

5. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Authentication: JWT tokens with 6-hour expiration
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions and authorization checks
  • Monitoring: Continuous security monitoring and threat detection
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Password Security: Bcrypt hashing with salt
